Originally a music recording studio having seen Nick Cave, Radiohead, and The Prodigy produce classics there, it continues to welcome many of the world's most talented musicians every day. The bar has been a Shoreditch fixture for nearly 20 years which makes it something of an institution in a part of town which has so dramatically changed over the past decade. The extensive selection of drinks and heavy emphasis on microbreweries makes it an ideal spot for craft beer lovers too. With many free entry events of live music and DJ nights there is certainly lots of fun to be had there. On the lower ground floor you will find a GARLANDO World Champion with overhead lighting and an arcade multi game system which makes ideal to have a few games of “beat them up” with your mates or maybe something mellower like Pang as a “co-op” game if you are out on a date. All machines accept £1 coins only. Strong Room is available for group bookings.
